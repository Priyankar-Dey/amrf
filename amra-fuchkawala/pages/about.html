Our Journey

From a Family Recipe to a City Favourite

It all started in our grandmother's kitchen — a secret recipe for tamarind water that made every fuchka unforgettable. What began as a small weekend stall has grown into one of the city's most beloved street food destinations.

আমরা Fuchkawala was founded with one simple belief: great street food deserves the same respect as any fine dish. Fresh ingredients, daily preparation, and genuine love for the craft — that's what separates us from the rest.

Today, we serve hundreds of customers every day, but every fuchka is still made with the same care as that very first plate.

Fresh ingredients sourced every morning, no exceptions
Original family recipe, unchanged since day one
Hygienically prepared — clean hands, clean stall, always

Our Journey

How We Got Here

Every great story starts small. Ours started with a steel pot of tamarind water and a grandmother's recipe that was too good to keep to ourselves.

2015
The First Stall
Started with just a small pushcart, 50 pieces of fuchka, and a dream. Sold out in under an hour.
2017
Growing Regulars
Word spread fast. We outgrew the pushcart and set up our permanent stall spot. Dahi fuchka was introduced.
2020
Surviving & Thriving
Despite the pandemic, our loyal customers made sure we bounced back stronger. New items added to the menu.
2025
আমরা Fuchkawala Online
Launched our website to connect with more food lovers. Serving 500+ customers daily and still counting.
